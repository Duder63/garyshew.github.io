About Me

I am currently a JavaScript Student at Eleven Fifty Academy. My goal to become skilled as a full-stack developer to enable a new level of application development ability to my UI and UX skillset and experience.

My name is Gary Shew and I am web designer who is currently learning software application development languages to enhance my ability to bring a website to life. I have helped promote company brands, message and products via the web. I can design from concept layout to function.
